Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Email Newsletters

When you subscribe to our email updates, we collect:

  • Your email address (required)
  • Your name (optional)
  • Subscription preferences and consent records

Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use our website. This collects:

  • Page views, session duration, and visitor counts
  • General location data (country/region/city level)
  • Referral sources, device information, and browser type
  • User interactions and website behavior patterns
  • These services use cookies and are subject to their respective privacy policies

Chat Support

Our website includes a chat widget (Tawk.to) for visitor support:

  • Chat conversations are stored for support purposes
  • Any information you share in chat is collected
  • Chat data is managed by our chat service provider

Advertising and Conversion Tracking

We use Facebook Pixel for advertising and conversion tracking:

  • Tracks website visits and actions for advertising purposes
  • Helps us measure the effectiveness of our fundraising campaigns
  • Subject to Facebook's privacy policies

How We Use Your Information

  • Email Updates: To send you program updates, fundraising progress, and news about our cycling initiatives
  • Analytics: To improve our website and understand visitor interests
  • Chat: To provide support and answer questions about our programs
  • Advertising: To reach potential supporters and track fundraising campaign effectiveness

Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe: You can unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in our messages
  • Data Access: Contact us to request information about data we have collected
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Disable chat or use ad blockers to limit tracking

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Donation/email service (Givebutter), analytics (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity), chat (Tawk.to), advertising (Facebook), fundraising platform (Givebutter)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Contact Us

  • Mail: Helmet Heads, 612 Joan St, Columbia, SC 29203

This privacy policy may be updated as our services evolve. Material changes will be communicated through our email list or website notices.
